Carousel

Our Carousel is a robust and versatile component that can be an image slider, to an item carousel, to an onboarding experience. It is touch enabled making it especially smooth to use on mobile.

Note: This is also touch compatible! Try swiping with your finger to scroll through the carousel.

Full Width Slider

Our carousel has a full width option that makes it into a simple and elegant image carousel. You can also have indicators that show up on the bottom of the slider.

Note: This is also touch compatible! Try swiping with your finger to scroll through the carousel.

Special Options

The carousel doesn't only support images but also allows you to make content carousels. You can add fixed items to your carousel by adding a div with the class carousel-fixed-item and adding your fixed content in there.

Note: This is also touch compatible! Try swiping with your finger to scroll through the carousel.

Kim So-hyun

Kim So-hyun, an Australian-born South Korean actress, gained fame for her roles in Moon Embracing the Sun (2012) and Missing You (2013).
The actress began her acting career in 2012 with Ma Boy and has since starred in various TV series, including Who Are You: School, Hey Ghost, Let's Fight, The Emperor: Owner of the Mask, Radio Romance, Love Alarm, and River Where the Moon Rises.

Song Hye-kyo

Song Hye-kyo is a South Korean actress known for her leading roles in television dramas and films. She gained international popularity through her roles in Autumn in My Heart, All In, Full House, Descendants of the Sun, Encounter, and The Glory. Her film work includes Hwang Jin Yi, The Grandmaster, My Brilliant Life, and The Queens.
Song Hye-kyo, a top Hallyu star, achieved international success with her television dramas, ranking 7th in Forbes' Korea Power Celebrity 40 list in 2017 and 6th in 2018.

Lee Ji-eun

TLee Ji-eun, also known as IU, is a South Korean singer-songwriter and actress. She debuted as a singer at fifteen with Lost and Found (2008). Her follow-up albums, Growing Up and IU...IM, gained mainstream success. However, her 2010 album Real's lead single "Good Day" achieved national stardom. The song spent five weeks at the top of South Korea's Gaon Digital Chart and was ranked number one on Billboard's "100 Greatest K-Pop Songs of the 2010s" list in 2019.

Jun Ji-hyun

Wang Ji-hyun (Korean: 왕지현; born 30 October 1981), known professionally as Jun Ji-hyun (전지현) or Gianna Jun, is a South Korean actress and model. She rose to fame with her role in the romantic comedy film My Sassy Girl (2001), which became a tremendous sensation across Asia and sparked an international breakthrough for Korean cinema.[2] Other notable films include Il Mare (2000), Windstruck (2004), The Thieves (2012), The Berlin File (2013) and Assassination (2015). She also starred in the popular television series My Love from the Star (2013–2014) and The Legend of the Blue Sea (2016–2017), as well as the Netflix series Kingdom (2021).
